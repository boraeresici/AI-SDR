# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and constructive environment for all contributors.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Give actionable, technical feedback.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Focus on ideas and implementation, not individuals.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination.
- Personal attacks, insults, or trolling.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions/comments that violate this policy.

## Reporting

For conduct concerns, open a private security-style report via repository maintainers and include:

1. Incident summary
2. Relevant links/screenshots
3. Date/time and context

All reports will be reviewed in good faith.
